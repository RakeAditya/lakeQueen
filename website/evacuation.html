Evacuation Procedure

1

General Emergency Signal

7 or more short blasts followed by one long blast

2

Action by Passengers on Hearing General Emergency Signal

When the 'General Emergency Signal' is heard, passengers should keep calm and remain seated and further wait for other arrangements/ instructions announced by the crew. The capacity of each life raft is 50 Passengers

3

Abondan Ship Signal

7 or more short blasts followed by one long blast

4

Action by Passengers on Hearing Abandon Ship Signal

When the 'General Emergency Signal' is heard, passengers should keep calm and go back to their own seats and take off their shoes. Put on the life jacket kept under the seats as per 'Life Jacket donning instructions'. Do not bring any sharp objects or luggage on the life raft. Do not rush onto the escape door. Follow the crew's instructions to proceed to respective life rafts
